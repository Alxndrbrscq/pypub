William Cember

Actuary. Consultant. Technology Enthusiast.

William (Bill) Cember is a consultant in the life insurance industry. Bill has traditional actuarial experience, previously working as an actuary at a major North American life insurer, and he is currently focused on financial modeling and more generally helping insurers improve their actuarial function as whole. Outside of work, Bill is interested in emerging technologies, both how they can improve society and our lives individually.
